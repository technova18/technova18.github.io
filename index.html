About

Concern for man and his fate must always form the chief interest of all technical endeavors. Never forget this in the midst of your diagrams and equations.

What is Technova?

Department of Computer Science in collaboration with Department of Electronics of Fakir Chand College presents Technova for the Year 2020 to be held from 20th February,2020 to 25th February, 2020. It is our 2nd Version of Technova : The Inter College Technical Fest. This year we are here with more than 25 events both in Online & Offline Mode. Visit our Events Section to get details.
