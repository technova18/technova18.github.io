Guidelines

Participation Rules: Any two students can make a group and register themselves in the same form for the technical events - Design the Globe (Web Design Contest), Digikit (Digital Circuits Design Contest), Code-Battle (Coding Contest), Mindbend (Technical Quiz Contest). From the group prepared, any one or both can join the event - Battle of Word (Debate Contest) individually.

Contest Details: If number of students attended for any technical event excluding Battle of Word (Debate Contest), became more than resources (Computer) available, then prelims will be taken. After the result of prelims selected candidates will contest for the Final Round. Any one can participate at the Battle of Word (Debate Contest) at any time within the scheduled time-period of the same event.

Timing Details : Our Technical Events - Design the Globe (Web Design Contest) and Digikit (Digital Circuit Design) will occur in parallel. After starting of Final Contest, Students who discarded at the time of Prelims, could join Battle of Word (Debate Contest). After Completion of first 2 events, Code-battle (Coding Contest) will start. Here also, after starting of Final, discarded students could join the Battle of Word (Debate Contest). Although any participant can join the event - Battle of Word (Debate Contest), without joining in any other technical events.

Seminar Participation : Any Participant registered for technical events, already counted as a seminar participant. So do not fill up the seminar registration form if you are filling up the technical event registration form.
